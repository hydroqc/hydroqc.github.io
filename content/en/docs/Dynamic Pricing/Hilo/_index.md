It is possible to use the Hydroqc project when you are subscribed to [Hilo](https://www.hydroquebec.com/residentiel/mieux-consommer/offres-pour-economiser-cet-hiver/service-maison-intelligente-hilo.html). In this case, you will retrieve your basic account information available in your portal.

For a more complete integration of the Hilo ecosystem, its challenges, and real-time consumption, we suggest using the Home-Assistant integration project [Hilo](https://github.com/dvd-dev/hilo).
